Lasagna

Description

A Lasagna wich is worth the efort
24h Lasagna

The Meatsauce

  • 3 carrots
  • 3 celery sticks
  • 2 onions
  • 1kg ground meat
  • 1 glas dried tomatos
  • 1.5l beef stock
  • 1.5l tomato sauce
  • 2tb tomato paste
  • 1 Glas of red wine

The Bechamelsauce

  • 100g Butter
  • 100g flour
  • 1.5l Milk
  • 100g of Parmesan
  • Pepper
  • nutmag
  • Salt

Steps

The Meatsauce

  1. cut your vegetables in 0.5cm thick cubs
  2. cock ur ground meat in a metal cooking pot untill it gets brow so it gets some flavor
  3. put the Meat aside and cock now ur vegetables in the cooking pot
  4. add some oliveoil and butter
  5. also add the dried Tomatos which you cut also in small pices also add the oil of them for flavor
  6. after a few minutes add the tomato paste and let it cook another few minutes
  7. Now add the meat back in
  8. now add the beef stock (if you can add only 500ml off it and let the water cook away do that 3 times)
  9. After that add the glas of wine and let it reduce
  10. After it has reduced add the tomato sauce
  11. Let it cook atleast 6h the longer the better

The Bechamelsauce

  1. Add the Butter in to the cooking pot and let it brown(browning Butter adds a nuty flavor to your sauce)>
  2. After your butter is lightly brown add your flour and let it cook for 1 or 2 Minutes so the flour tast disappears
  3. Now add little by little some Milk so it doesn`t get clupy
  4. After you poured all the milk in add your parmesan add let it cook for a few minutes
  5. Now add your spices (pepper, nutmag and salt)
